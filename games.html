The Hunger Within

A tale of hunger in the dark and a mother's love

A dark fantasy 3D turn-based tactical SRPG where a cursed mother, transformed into a werewolf, fights for the prosperity of her species and the future of her children.

Play Demo

Story

The Cursed Mother's Tale. A mother becomes a werewolf due to a curse. Her desire was for her species to prosper, but for some unknown reason, her children are born with traits of the enemies she consumes.

She realized that to prosper, she must continue to fight and consume. She resents the curse, but her children are innocent.

She suffers from the cannibalistic curse inflicted by a demon. She devours her enemies, lays eggs, and her offspring inherit the enemies' traits (e.g., wolf claws, snake venom).

While hating the curse, she loves her children and fights to defeat the demon and break the curse. Experience a story of tactical combat, child-rearing, and the interweaving of hope and despair across 12 stages.

Gameplay

Tactical Combat System. Players control the mother wolf and her offspring on a 10x10 3D grid, progressing through the game by defeating enemies or achieving stage objectives.

Each character has unique skills and abilities, requiring strategic combat that utilizes terrain and positioning. When the mother wolf defeats an enemy, she can consume it and give birth to offspring with that enemy's traits.

Child-Rearing System. Between stages, you can train your offspring and enhance their inherited traits. In certain stages, the children will come of age, and their growth will be depicted as the mother watches over them.

Each stage offers different objectives and challenges, requiring players to manage limited resources and devise optimal strategies.

Game Features

Tactical Combat

Strategic combat on a 10x10 3D grid. Deep tactical gameplay utilizing terrain and positioning. Diverse combat styles leveraging each character's traits.

Child-Rearing System

A system for birthing and raising offspring with traits gained from consuming enemies. Enhance abilities through child growth and training. Unique bond system influencing the mother-child relationship in the story.

Deep Story

A profound dark fantasy intertwining curse and maternal love. Multiple endings influenced by player choices. Relatable characters' conflicts and growth.

Diverse Stages

12 diverse stages reflecting the dark fantasy world, such as dark forests and blood-stained altars. Unique objectives and challenges for each stage. Changing weather and time of day impacting strategy.

Evolving Gameplay

New abilities and challenges unlocked as the game progresses. Enemy AI that adapts to player strategies. Diverse routes to completion enhancing replayability.

Immersive Presentation

Beautiful visuals and effects built with Godot 4.x. Atmospheric soundtrack enhancing tension. Dramatic presentations and cutscenes at key moments.
