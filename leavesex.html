üìñ Village Tales System

The "Village Tales" panel on the right side presents evolving narratives that change with each civilization stage. These stories depict the daily lives of people and how leaf distribution affects society, from simple tribal disputes to complex modern social issues.

Stories update periodically and become more sophisticated as civilization advances, offering insights into the human condition across different eras.

üéØ Strategy & Tips

üí° Effective Playing Strategies

Target Autumn: Autumn provides the highest leaf spawn rate and strongest wind effects, making it the most efficient season for collection.
Regional Balance: Avoid concentrating all leaves in one area. Spread them across multiple regions for balanced development.
Wind Timing: Apply wind when leaves are just beginning to fall for maximum guidance effectiveness.
Seasonal Adaptation: Adjust your strategy based on seasonal characteristics - be patient in winter, aggressive in autumn.
Read the Stories: Village tales provide hints about which regions need attention and reflect current social dynamics.
Long-term Planning: Consider that leaves decompose over time, so maintain consistent collection efforts rather than sporadic bursts.
