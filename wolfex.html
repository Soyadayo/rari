‚öîÔ∏è Gameplay Elements

Hunting System

Combat automatically starts when moving to an enemy's tile. Attack with Mother Wolf or pack to gain experience and hunger when defeating enemies.

Pack System

When Mother Wolf defeats an enemy, a new wolf pup with that enemy's trait joins the pack. The pack can act independently and perform powerful group attacks.

üí° Survival Tips

  • Health decreases when hunger reaches 0
  • Use howl to restore health for Mother Wolf and pack
  • Obstacle destruction ability unlocks at Level 3
  • Use pack traits strategically in combat

üìä Status Management

Mother Wolf Stats

HP: Health. Game over when it reaches 0

Hunger: Decreases over time. Health drops when 0

Attack: Damage dealt in combat

Pack Stats

Total Wolves: Size of the pack

Pack HP: Combined health of all members

Total Attack: Combined attack power of the pack

Traits: List of inherited abilities
